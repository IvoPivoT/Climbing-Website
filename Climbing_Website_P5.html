Climbing Workout Tips

Depending on your climbing, your workout should include some of these factors:

  1. Whole Body
  2. Strenght
  3. Endurance
  4. Coordination

Examples of workout gear:

  • Weights
  • Pull-up bar/Beastmaker
  • Slings
